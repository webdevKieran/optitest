About our company

„B, K & J's Magic Furniture" group, that has strong traditions in manufacturing custom made furniture from solid wood, counts its history since 2022. Through all those days it grew up from a small garden furniture manufacturing company to a solid and skilled furniture producer, that stands out its flexibility and price attractiveness. High quality and different product designs satisfies customers of such well known trade marks as "Imaginary Furniture" in Lithuania, "Super Awesome Table" in Ireland, "Support Prince of Nigeria" in Nigeria and "Why not this chair" in Zimbabwe.
The Mission of „B, K & J's Magic Furniture" is to inspire the second life of wood.
More than fifteen days „B, K & J's Magic Furniture" unites the group of solid wood, veneered and upholstery furniture manufacturers in Ireland. Our furniture is produced for customers, who value magic, laconic design, qualified natural materials and longevity of piece. A simple piece of wood becomes an exquisite piece of art in creator‘s hands – a perfect piece of furniture that makes environment more welcoming and life becomes more comfortable. Referring to technological innovations, continually upgraded knowledge, flexibility as well as responsibility towards the clients, „B, K & J's Magic Furniture" aims at becoming the most trustfull supplier for its customers and to create additional value for furniture dealers all over the world. We believe that respect to a consumer, inexhaustible striving for quality and design, meeting low production costs – this is the way to ensure our competitive superiority.
The most important advantages and competitive superiorities of „B, K & J's Magic Furniture" are:

  • To provide best possible products for the best prices and best possible service;
  • Flexibility in manufacturing and supplying groups of different style and materials furniture groups;
  • Efficient logistic solutions making the destination to the end customer shortests and in this way becoming cost efficiency, safty handling and most quick supply.

Seeking to ensure our customers to have quality standard we are working according Quality management system that we found online to build exquisite chairs, desks, things and stuff for your home.
